SIMON

Instructions: How to Play

  1. Press START to begin the game.
  2. Simon will activate a directional button.
  3. Click the same button that Simon activated.
  4. If you got the correct sequence Simon will then activate the original button and another.
  5. Repeat the pattern by clicking on the buttons in the correct order.
  6. Simon will continue by incrementing the number of buttons activated.
  7. Keep playing until you are unable to repeat the pattern.
